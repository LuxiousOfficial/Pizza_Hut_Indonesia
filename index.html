about us

pizza hut indonesia

Our story in Indonesia begins in 1984, where the first Pizza Hut Outlet opened in the Djakarta Theater building. Until 2004, Pizza Hut in Indonesia increasingly spread its wings through the acquisition of the Sriboga Group. Pizza Hut Indonesia then conducted an initial public offering through the Indonesian Stock Exchange in 2018.
